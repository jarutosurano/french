# Week 1: French Sounds & First Words

**Goal:** Learn French sounds, master être/avoir, build your first 100 words.

---

## Day 1 — Setup & The Alphabet

**Morning (~15 min)**

- [ ] Watch: [French Alphabet Pronunciation](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=French+Alphabet+Pronunciation+FrenchPod101) — listen and repeat each letter
- Pay attention to: **E** ("uh"), **G** ("zhay"), **J** ("zhee"), **R** (back of throat), **U** (lips say "oo", tongue says "ee")

**Evening (~30 min)**

- [ ] Watch: [French greetings for beginners](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=learn+french+greetings+beginners) — pick any short video
- [ ] Practice saying 3x each: **Bonjour** (bone-JOOR), **Merci** (mare-C), **Au revoir** (oh-ruh-VWOR)

**Before bed**

- [ ] Say all three greetings out loud one more time

---

## Day 2 — Greetings

**Morning (~15 min)**

- [ ] Review the [Day 2 word list](week1-words.md#day-2-greetings) — read each word and say it out loud 3x

**Evening (~30 min)**

- [ ] Watch: [French greetings — Comme Une Française](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=french+greetings+comme+une+francaise)
- [ ] Practice introducing yourself out loud: "Bonjour, je m'appelle _____. Comment allez-vous?"
- [ ] Go through the [Day 2 words](week1-words.md#day-2-greetings) again — cover the "Meaning" column and test yourself

## Day 3 — Subject Pronouns + Être (to be)

**Morning (~15 min)**

- [ ] Review the [subject pronouns table](week1-words.md#day-3-subject-pronouns-etre) — say each one 3x

**Evening (~30 min)**

- [ ] Study the [être conjugation](week1-words.md#day-3-subject-pronouns-etre) — write it out on paper, say each form 3x
- [ ] Practice these sentences out loud:
    - "Je suis _____" (I am _____)
    - "Il est grand" (He is tall)
    - "Elle est intelligente" (She is smart)
    - "Nous sommes contents" (We are happy)

**Before bed**

- [ ] Try to say all 6 être forms from memory (je suis, tu es, il est...)

---

## Day 4 — Avoir (to have)

**Morning (~15 min)**

- [ ] Review [avoir conjugation](week1-words.md#day-4-avoir-to-have) — say each form 3x

**Evening (~30 min)**

- [ ] Write 5 sentences mixing être and avoir. Templates:
    1. "Je suis _______ et j'ai _______ ans." (I am _____ and I am _____ years old.)
    2. "Elle est gentille et elle a un chat." (She is kind and she has a cat.)
    3. "Nous sommes contents." (We are happy.)
    4. "Ils ont une maison." (They have a house.)
    5. "Tu es intelligent et tu as un livre." (You are smart and you have a book.)

**Before bed**

- [ ] From memory: conjugate être then avoir out loud

---

## Day 5 — Articles + First Podcast

**Morning (~15 min)**

- [ ] Review [definite articles](week1-words.md#day-5-articles) (le, la, l', les)

**Evening (~30 min)**

- [ ] Listen: [Coffee Break French Season 1 Episode 1](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=coffee+break+french+season+1+episode+1) — just listen, don't stress about understanding everything
- [ ] Study the gender rules: **-tion, -ée, -ance** = feminine / **-age, -ment, -eau** = masculine

**Before bed**

- [ ] Point at 5 objects around you and say "le" or "la" + the French word (use Google Translate to look them up)

---

## Day 6 — Indefinite Articles + Negation

**Morning (~15 min)**

- [ ] Review [indefinite articles & negation](week1-words.md#day-6-indefinite-articles-negation) (un, une, des + ne...pas)

**Evening (~45 min)**

- [ ] Watch: [French negation — Comme Une Française](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=comme+une+francaise+french+negation)
- [ ] Watch: [Être & Avoir conjugation](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=francais+avec+pierre+etre+avoir+conjugation)
- [ ] Write 6 negative sentences:
    1. Je ne suis pas américain. (I am not American.)
    2. Elle n'a pas de chien. (She doesn't have a dog.)
    3. Nous ne sommes pas fatigués. (We are not tired.)
    4. Il n'est pas grand. (He is not tall.)
    5. Tu n'as pas de voiture. (You don't have a car.)
    6. Make up your own!
- [ ] Read all 6 sentences out loud slowly

---

## Day 7 — Review Day

**Morning (~30 min)**

- [ ] Without looking, write out on paper:
    - All 9 subject pronouns
    - Full être conjugation
    - Full avoir conjugation
    - The articles (le, la, l', les, un, une, des)
    - Negation rule (ne + verb + pas)
- [ ] Check your answers against the [Week 1 Words](week1-words.md) page

**Afternoon (~30 min)**

- [ ] Write 10 sentences using everything from this week (use the templates from the week)
- [ ] Watch any French beginner video on YouTube that looks fun — just for immersion

**Before bed**

- [ ] Say from memory: "Bonjour, je m'appelle _____. J'ai _____ ans. Je ne suis pas français. Merci, au revoir!"

---

## Week 1 Checklist

By end of Day 7, you should be able to:

- [ ] Recognize French letter sounds
- [ ] Greet someone (bonjour, bonsoir, salut, comment allez-vous)
- [ ] Introduce yourself (je m'appelle..., j'ai ... ans)
- [ ] Conjugate **être** without looking
- [ ] Conjugate **avoir** without looking
- [ ] Know when to use le/la/l'/les and un/une/des
- [ ] Make a sentence negative with ne...pas
